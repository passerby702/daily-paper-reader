## 摘要
化学反应网络（CRN）对于理解反应机理和指导化学合成至关重要，然而计算探索仍受限于化学空间的组合增长、反应路径筛选的可靠性以及热力学和动力学性质评估的成本。在此，我们提出ByteCRN，一个端到端的计算CRN探索框架，它将化学知识驱动的反应枚举与生成式过渡态建模相结合。我们框架的一个关键组成部分是一个用于过渡态生成和反应验证的生成式整流流架构，它将反应物-产物对映射到候选过渡态结构，并通过映射回反应物和产物来验证连通性。这种统一的生成策略取代了传统计算工作流中最昂贵的步骤，即迭代过渡态搜索和内禀反应坐标验证，在一个完整的CRN构建流程中实现。ByteCRN相比传统工作流实现了10-100倍的加速，同时保持了对单个反应的高预测保真度。在网络规模上，它有效地剪除了约70-90%的枚举反应，简化了复杂反应空间的探索。通过涉及氰基乙醛的新颖路径的发现以及对具有挑战性的γ-酮基氢过氧化物网络的成功建模，展示了其实用性和可扩展性，为自主化学探索提供了一种实用的、可扩展的方法。

## Abstract
Chemical reaction networks (CRNs) are crucial for understanding reaction mechanisms and guiding chemical synthesis, yet the computational exploration remains limited by the combinatorial growth of chemical space, the reliability of reaction path screening, and the cost of evaluating thermodynamic and kinetic properties. Here, we present ByteCRN, an end-to-end framework for computational CRN exploration that combines chemically informed reaction enumeration with generative transition state modeling. A key component of our framework is a generative rectified flow architecture for both transition state generation and reaction validation, where it maps reactant-product pairs to candidate transition state structures and verifies connectivity by mapping back to reactants and products. This unified generative strategy replaces the most expensive steps of conventional computational workflows, namely iterative transition state search and intrinsic reaction coordinate validation, within a complete CRN construction pipeline. ByteCRN delivers a 10--100-fold acceleration over traditional workflows while maintaining high predictive fidelity for individual reactions. At the network scale, it effectively prunes $\sim$70-90% of the enumerated reactions, streamlining the exploration of complex reaction space. Its utility is illustrated through the discovery of novel pathways involving cyanoacetaldehyde and the successful modeling of the challenging $γ$-ketohydroperoxide network, demonstrating a practical, scalable approach to autonomous chemical exploration.
